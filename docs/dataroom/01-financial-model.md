# Sold Direct — 5-Year Financial Model (Prime · 1% Fee · AI-lean · Bank Sponsor)

All figures **ZAR**. Y1 = first full year of Cape Town operations. Illustrative; rests on the
sourced assumptions in `00-assumptions.md`. **Strategy:** prime areas (Atlantic Seaboard, City
Bowl, upper Southern Suburbs, Constantia; then prime JHB-north and Umhlanga), run by a small
**AI-augmented** team.

## 1. The monetisation model (three legally-clean lines)

1. **Origination referral (free 0% path).** Free requires a **qualifying ≥80% bond via our
   multi-bank originator (ooba) + our conveyancer**. The buyer keeps **full rate-choice across
   banks**; we earn ~0.5% of the bond. *No single-bank exclusivity — that mechanic is legally grey
   (steering / competition / PPRA), so we avoid it.*
2. **1% facilitation fee (everyone else).** Cash buyers, sub-80% bonds, or buyers who won't use our
   partners pay **1% of price** — still ~6× cheaper than 5–7% (+VAT) agent commission.
3. **Bank headline sponsorship.** A bank pays an **annual fee** to be the featured brand on the
   platform — advertising to a prime-property audience, **not** steering. High-margin; scales with
   reach.

| Per R6.5m prime deal | Free path (≥80% bond, any bank) | Non-partner path |
|---|---|---|
| Sold Direct earns | 0.5% × ~R5.85m bond → **~R29k** | 1% × R6.5m → **~R65k** |
| Consumer cost | **R0** | 1% (vs 5–7% agent) |

## 2. The revenue engine (base drivers)

```
Revenue/deal = [ bonded% × 0.5% × bond ] + [ (1−bonded%) × 1% × price ]   ← referral OR fee
             + conveyancing (~R9–10k) + add-ons (~R5–6.5k)
Total revenue = deals × revenue/deal  +  annual bank sponsorship
```

## 6. Watch-outs (flag before investors do)

1. **High margins** (AI-lean + 1% take + sponsorship) — present a reinvest-for-growth case.
2. **AI agents at 50% of roles in Y1** — operationally aggressive; validate with a pilot.
3. **Sponsorship scales with real audience** — keep Y1 conservative until reach is proven.
4. **Marketing-claim consistency** — "0%" holds for the qualifying-bond path; disclose the 1% fee
   for the non-partner path clearly (it's the Flex tier).
