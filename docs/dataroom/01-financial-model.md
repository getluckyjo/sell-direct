# Sold Direct — 5-Year Financial Model (Reinvest-for-Growth)

All figures **ZAR**. Y1 = first full year of Cape Town operations. Illustrative; rests on the
sourced assumptions in `00-assumptions.md`. **Strategy:** prime areas (Atlantic Seaboard, City
Bowl, upper Southern Suburbs, Constantia; then prime JHB-north and Umhlanga), an **AI-augmented**
team, and **reinvestment of the high gross margin into above-the-line (brand) marketing** to drive
the deal ramp.

## 1. The monetisation model (three legally-clean lines)

1. **Origination referral (free 0% path).** Free requires a **qualifying ≥80% bond via our
   multi-bank originator (ooba) + our conveyancer**. Buyer keeps **full rate-choice**; we earn
   ~0.5% of the bond. *No single-bank exclusivity (legally grey — steering / competition / PPRA).*
2. **1% facilitation fee.** Cash / sub-80% / non-partner deals pay **1% of price** — ~6× cheaper
   than 5–7% (+VAT) agent commission.
3. **Bank headline sponsorship.** A bank pays an **annual fee** (R3m → R8m) to be the featured
   brand on the platform — advertising, not steering.

## 2. The revenue engine (base drivers)

```
Revenue/deal = [ bonded% × 0.5% × bond ] + [ (1−bonded%) × 1% × price ] + conveyancing + add-ons
Total revenue = deals × revenue/deal  +  annual bank sponsorship
```

## 6. The database — optional ecosystem revenue (the moat)

Owning the homeowner relationship + data unlocks a **referral menu worth ~R50,000 of ecosystem
revenue per customer** — insurance, solar, moving, fibre, security, life cover, estate planning and
more (full table on the **Ancillary Revenue** tab of the workbook). This is **modelled as optional**
(default **off**) so the headline P&L stays conservative; it demonstrates the expansion-revenue
upside and the defensibility of the data asset.

| | Value |
|---|---|
| Full ecosystem potential per customer (sum of midpoints) | **~R49,625** |
| Realistic **additional** captured / deal (~10% blended, net of items already in core) | **~R4,980** |
| If toggled **on**: uplift to Y5 base revenue (2,900 × R4,980) | **+R14.4m** |

Top contributors per deal (midpoint × attach): life cover ~R900, homeowners insurance ~R825,
home-improvement referrals ~R680, solar ~R625. **Flip `Assumptions!C61` to 1** to fold it into the
P&L. The point for investors: **each registered deal is the start of a multi-year, multi-product
relationship — the transaction is the customer-acquisition event, not the whole revenue.**

## 7. Watch-outs (flag before investors do)

1. **ATL→deals conversion** is the central growth assumption — validate with a pilot before
   committing the R50m/yr spend; it's the Purplebricks failure mode if CAC runs away.
2. **AI agents at 50% of roles** — operationally aggressive; pilot it.
3. **Sponsorship scales with real audience** — R3m in Y1 assumes a launch-partner deal is secured.
4. **Marketing-claim consistency** — "0%" holds for the qualifying-bond path; disclose the 1% fee
   for the non-partner path (it's the Flex tier).
