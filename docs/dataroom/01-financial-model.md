# Sold Direct — 5-Year Financial Model (Prime · Nedbank-0% · 1% Fee · AI-lean)

All figures **ZAR**. Y1 = first full year of Cape Town operations. Illustrative; rests on the
sourced assumptions in `00-assumptions.md`. **Strategy:** prime areas (Atlantic Seaboard, City
Bowl, upper Southern Suburbs, Constantia; then prime JHB-north and Umhlanga), run by a lean,
**AI-augmented** team.

## 1. The monetisation rule

Every deal pays a take-rate of value, via one of two paths:

- **Free 0% — requires a ≥80% bond with our partner bank (Nedbank) + our panel conveyancer.**
  We earn the **Nedbank kickback** (% of the bond) — consumer pays nothing.
- **Otherwise a 1% facilitation fee** on the price (cash buyers / sub-80% bonds / buyers who
  won't use our partners). Still **~6× cheaper** than 5–7% (+VAT) agent commission.

| Per R6.5m prime deal | Free path (≥80% Nedbank bond) | Non-partner path |
|---|---|---|
| Mechanism | Nedbank kickback (bank pays) | 1% facilitation fee (buyer pays) |
| Sold Direct earns | 0.5% × ~R5.85m bond → **~R29k** | 1% × R6.5m → **~R65k** |
| Consumer cost | **R0** | 1% (vs 5–7% agent) |

> The 1% non-partner fee is the **largest revenue component** and makes the model robust to the
> cash/bond mix. **Strategic caveat:** tying 0% to one bank limits buyer rate-choice — secure the
> Nedbank agreement + PPRA/legal sign-off, and keep a multi-bank fallback.

## 2. The revenue engine (base drivers)

```
Revenue/deal = [ bonded% × 0.5% × bond ]  +  [ (1−bonded%) × 1% × price ]   ← kickback OR fee
             + conveyancing referral (~R9–10k)  +  add-ons (~R5–6.5k)
```

## 3. The AI-lean operating model

**50% of headcount are AI agents** (concierge follow-ups, document chasing, status updates,
coordination), costed at **~R0.15m/yr each** (LLM API + tooling + human oversight) vs.
**~R0.70–0.78m/yr** for a fully-loaded human. This roughly **halves people-cost**:

## 6. Watch-outs (flag before investors do)

1. **High margins** (AI-lean + 1% take) — show a reinvest-for-growth case.
2. **Nedbank-exclusive 0%** — consumer-choice / PPRA / competition angles; needs the bank
   agreement and legal sign-off, plus a multi-bank fallback.
3. **AI agents at 50% of roles in Y1** — operationally aggressive; validate with a pilot before
   relying on it for the cost base.
4. **Marketing-claim consistency** — "0%" holds for the Nedbank path; disclose the 1% fee for the
   non-partner path clearly (it's the Flex tier).
