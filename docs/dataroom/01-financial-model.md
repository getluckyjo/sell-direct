# Sold Direct — 5-Year Financial Model (Prime-led, Tapering to Upper-Market)

All figures **ZAR**. Y1 = first full year of Cape Town operations. Illustrative; rests on the
sourced assumptions in `00-assumptions.md`. **Strategy:** *land at prime* (R6.5m, biggest rand
savings + bond) and **taper the average price down as the brand scales** — broadening into the
upper-market — to grow from a **much larger addressable pool** and cut the implied market-share
risk. Run by a small **AI-augmented** team with **above-the-line (brand) marketing** driving the
deal ramp.

## 1. The monetisation model (legally clean)

1. **Origination referral (free 0% path)** — qualifying ≥80% bond via our multi-bank originator
   (ooba) + our conveyancer; buyer keeps rate choice; we earn ~0.5% of the bond.
2. **1% facilitation fee** on cash / sub-80% / non-partner deals (~6× cheaper than agent commission).
3. **Bank headline sponsorship** — annual fee (R3m → R8m).
4. **Optional ecosystem revenue** (the database; default off — see Ancillary Revenue tab).

## 2. Price taper & the revenue engine

The **average transacting price tapers from R6.5m (Y1) to R4.0m (Y5)** — we start prime and
broaden down-market. This lowers revenue per deal **but** expands the addressable segment from the
~14k prime (>R5m) pool to a ~35k upper-market (>~R4m) pool, dropping the implied Y5 share from
**~21% to ~8%** (see `03-market-sizing.md`).

| | Y1 | Y2 | Y3 | Y4 | Y5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Avg transacting price** | **R6.5m** | **R5.75m** | **R5.0m** | **R4.5m** | **R4.0m** |
| Bonded share (≥80%) | 40% | 40% | 40% | 42% | 42% |
| **Revenue per deal** | **R64,700** | **R58,850** | **R54,000** | **R50,605** | **R47,260** |
| Registered deals | 50 | 220 | 650 | 1,500 | 2,900 |
| Bank sponsorship (Rm/yr) | 3.0 | 4.0 | 5.0 | 6.5 | 8.0 |

## 5. Watch-outs (flag before investors do)

1. **ATL→deals conversion** — the central growth assumption; validate with a CT pilot.
2. **Upper-market pool size** (~35k) — an estimate; confirm. The taper makes the share far safer
   (~8%) but the pool count still needs grounding.
3. **AI agents at 50% of roles** — pilot before relying on it for the cost base.
4. **Marketing-claim consistency** — "0%" holds for the qualifying-bond path; disclose the 1% fee.
